Welcome

Serving Greater Louisville since 1995.

Al Nur Masjid, located in the heart of Greater Louisville, is a vibrant and inclusive space for Muslims from diverse backgrounds. Established in 2008, our mosque has grown to become a beacon of faith, education, and community support.

We offer daily prayers, weekly educational sessions, youth and sisters programs, Quran learning, community meals, and opportunities to grow together in worship, manners, and beneficial knowledge.
