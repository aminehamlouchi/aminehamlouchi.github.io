Now

Start simple. Make it feel intentional.

This first version is built around the information that is actually known: your name, the plan to go live on GitHub, and a preference for a site with movement, polish, and personality instead of a blank resume shell.

The project space is intentionally open. When you have projects, writing, photos, social links, or a real resume PDF, this layout already has places for them without needing to start over.

Principles

Polish counts

Small interactions, responsive spacing, and precise typography do a lot of quiet work.

Live beats perfect

The site is structured for GitHub Pages now, then ready to evolve as your story fills in.

Room for depth

Intro today, resume next, and future projects without forcing fake content into the page.
